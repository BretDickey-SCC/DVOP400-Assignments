Centralized Workflow

This page describes a centralized workflow approach for managing tasks and projects. In a centralized workflow, all tasks and communications are funneled through a single point of control, allowing for streamlined decision-making and oversight. This method is particularly effective for teams that require tight coordination and clear accountability.
